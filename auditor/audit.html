How do we comply with HIPAA?

We follow the rules strictly in every detail.

Limited information

We only collect limited information that is not prohibit by HIPAA.

Clear doubts just in time

We provide detailed explanation for each field at the time we collect the data.

Privacy consents

We ask user to give consents on every operations and we respect the every choice from our customer.

Agreements

We separate the terms of use, privacy policies and pharmacy privacy policies into three different sections and we allow customer to dis-agree with any one of them.

Clear and easy to read

We revised our Privacy Policy and make it easier to read and clearer to understand.

Data download
